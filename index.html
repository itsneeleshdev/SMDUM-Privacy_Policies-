SMDUM Privacy Policy

Smart Mobile Data Usage Manager – A lightweight, privacy-first Android app

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Thank you for using SMDUM — a simple tool designed to help you understand and control your mobile and Wi-Fi data usage.

Our core commitment: SMDUM is built to be completely local-first. We do not collect, store, transmit, or monetize any of your personal data. Everything happens on your device — nothing is sent to us or any third party.

1. What SMDUM Is and How It Works

SMDUM is a personal data usage monitor for Android. It uses official Android system APIs to show you how much mobile data and Wi-Fi each app on your device is using. All processing, calculations, and storage occur locally — there are no servers, no cloud sync, and no analytics SDKs involved.

2. Information We Access (and Don’t Access)

We want to be very clear: SMDUM does not collect any personally identifiable information. We never ask for or access your name, email, phone number, contacts, messages, photos, location, microphone, camera, or any other personal content.

What is accessed (and stays only on your device)

  • App-level mobile data and Wi-Fi usage statistics provided by Android
  • Timestamps and aggregated usage summaries (hourly / daily / monthly)
  • Basic app metadata: package names, user-facing app names, and icons (used only to make charts readable)

All of this information comes directly from Android’s built-in UsageStatsManager and NetworkStatsManager APIs. It never leaves your device.

3. Permissions We Request – and Why

SMDUM only asks for the minimum permissions strictly necessary for its functionality. Here’s exactly what we need and why:

Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)

Allows the app to read system usage statistics so it can show accurate per-app data consumption breakdowns.

Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)

Helps distinguish between mobile data and Wi-Fi connections when displaying usage.

Foreground Service (if enabled)

Used only when you activate real-time alerts or monitoring features — ensures notifications work reliably in the background.

We never request dangerous or unrelated permissions (location, contacts, storage, camera, etc.).

4. How Your Data Is Used

The information accessed is used solely for these purposes — all on-device:

  • Generating clear, visual charts and breakdowns of your data usage
  • Enabling custom alerts when you approach your data limit
  • Providing historical trends to help you manage consumption better

We do not sell, share, rent, profile, or monetize your data in any way.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored exclusively in your device’s local storage (SQLite database)
  • No data is ever uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • When you uninstall SMDUM, all app data is automatically removed by Android
  • We apply standard Android security practices (encrypted local storage where applicable)

6. Third Parties & Advertising

SMDUM contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics libraries (Firebase, AppsFlyer, etc.), no crash reporting tools, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any data from children.

8. Your Rights and Controls

Because everything stays on your device, you have full control:

  • Revoke any permission at any time through Android Settings → Apps → SMDUM
  • Clear app data or cache to reset all statistics
  • Uninstall the app to remove everything instantly

9. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this policy to reflect new features or Android changes. The updated version will always be available here with a new effective date. We recommend checking back periodically.
