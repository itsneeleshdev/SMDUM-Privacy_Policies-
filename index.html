SMDUM Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Welcome to SMDUM (Smart Mobile Data Usage Monitor). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, how it is used, and the choices you have when using the SMDUM mobile application.

Core principle: SMDUM is a local-first app. Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

1. Overview

SMDUM is a data usage analytics utility that helps users understand and manage mobile and Wi-Fi data consumption on their Android device using official Android system APIs.

2. Information We Collect

SMDUM does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, messages, photos, or precise location.

a. App Usage & Data Consumption (Local Only)

  • Mobile and Wi-Fi data usage per app
  • Hourly, daily, and monthly usage summaries
  • System-provided usage timestamps

This information is accessed using official Android APIs, processed entirely on your device, and never transmitted externally.

b. App Metadata

  • Installed app package names
  • App display names and icons

This data is used only to display readable analytics to you.

3. Permissions Explanation

• Usage Access Permission

Required to read system usage statistics so SMDUM can present accurate app-level data usage analytics.

• Network State Access

Used to distinguish between mobile data and Wi-Fi usage.

• Foreground Service (if applicable)

Used to ensure reliable tracking during active monitoring or alert scenarios.

SMDUM does not request permissions unrelated to its core functionality.

4. How We Use Information

  • Displaying charts and usage summaries
  • Providing user-defined data usage alerts
  • Improving on-device analytics accuracy

We do not sell, share, rent, or monetize your data in any form.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

6. Third-Party Services

SMDUM does not integrate with advertising SDKs, analytics services, trackers, or third-party data processors.

7. Children’s Privacy

SMDUM is a general utility app and does not target children. We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13.

8. User Control & Choices

  • Permissions can be revoked at any time via Android settings
  • App data can be cleared to reset analytics
  • Uninstalling the app fully removes all information

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
