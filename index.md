Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Welcome to SMDUM (Smart Mobile Data Usage Monitor). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when using the SMDUM mobile application.


---

1. Overview

SMDUM is a local-first data usage analytics app designed to help users understand and manage mobile data consumption on their device. We are committed to transparency, minimal data access, and full user control.

Key principle: Your data stays on your device.


---

2. Information We Collect

SMDUM does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may access the following device-level information, strictly for core functionality:

a. App Usage & Data Consumption (Local Only)

Mobile data usage statistics per app

Wi‑Fi vs mobile data usage breakdown

Usage timestamps (hourly / daily / monthly summaries)


This information:

Is retrieved using official Android system APIs

Is processed entirely on your device

Is never transmitted off your device


b. App Metadata

Installed app package names

App display names and icons


Used only to present readable analytics to you.


---

3. Permissions Explanation

SMDUM may request the following permissions:

• Usage Access Permission

Required to read system-provided usage statistics so the app can display accurate data usage analytics.

• Network State Access

Used to differentiate between mobile data and Wi‑Fi usage.

• Foreground Service (if applicable)

Used to ensure reliable data tracking during active monitoring sessions.

> SMDUM does not request permissions unrelated to its core purpose.




---

4. How We Use Information

All accessed data is used only for:

Displaying usage charts and summaries

Generating alerts based on user-defined thresholds

Improving on-device analytics accuracy


We do not:

Sell data

Share data with third parties

Use data for advertising or profiling



---

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device

No cloud storage or external servers are used

Clearing app data or uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored information


We follow Android platform best practices to ensure data isolation and security.


---

6. Third-Party Services

SMDUM does not integrate with:

Analytics SDKs

Advertising networks

External tracking services


If future versions introduce optional third-party services, this policy will be updated accordingly.


---

7. Children’s Privacy

SMDUM is a utility application and does not target children.

We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13.


---

8. User Control & Choices

You are always in control:

You can revoke permissions at any time via Android system settings

You can reset all analytics by clearing app data

You can uninstall the app to fully remove all stored information

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
